Name Anthony Wright
Location Philadelphia
Charge Rape and murder of his 77-year-old Nicetown neighbor in 1991.
Convicted 1993
Sentence Life without parole
Exonerated 2016, after DNA tests proved Wright was not the rapist.
False testimony Three of the five witnesses from Wright’s 1993 trial who made statements that Wright had entered the victim’s house recanted their statements at the 2016 retrial. The other two witnesses had died.
